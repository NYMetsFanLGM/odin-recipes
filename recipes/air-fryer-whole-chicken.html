Air Fryer Whole Chicken

Description

This is a whole chicken I cooked in my Air Fryer. This was the max size I could fit in the my air fryer at 4.75 lbs.

I found the recipe from this site Recipe site but changed up the ingredients I used, which I'll list below.

for my air fryer, the max time I can set it for is 60 minutes so if your air fryer does the same thing, set a timer for it to run for 3 minutes to warm up. When the timer finishes, put the chicken in the air fryer and set the timer for 60 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 4.5 - 4.75 lb. whole chicken
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon garlic salt
  • 1 teasppon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon Goya Adobo all purpose seasoning with pepper
  • 2 packets Goya Sason seasoning
  • non stick cooking spray

Instructions

  1. mix up all the ingredients inside a bowl except for the cooking spray
  2. set the air fryer for 3 minutes at 360 degrees for it to heat up
  3. after heating up the air fryer for 3 minutes, set the timer to 60 minutes at 360 degrees
  4. remove the backset, spray the inside with non-stick spray and place the chicken breast side down
  5. put backet back in air fryer and start the fryer for 50 minutes
  6. with 10 minutes remaining, pause the fryer and remove the basket
  7. flip the chicken over, place backet back in air fryer, and cook for 10 more minutes
