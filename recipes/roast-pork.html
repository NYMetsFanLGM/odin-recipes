Roast Pork

Description

This is some roast pork I made previously at home. before cooking in the oven, I prepared it overnight.

Ingredients:

  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 2 teaspoons ground pepper
  • 2 tablespoon adobo with pepper
  • 2 packets of Sason seasoning

Instructions

  1. prepare the garlic powder, oregano, and ground pepper in abowl
  2. place the olive oil on top of the ingredients and mix until the ingredients stick together
  3. cut 1 inch holes within the meat and cut in between the skin and the meat to loosen the skin
  4. place the mixed incredients in the open holes and use any leftover ingredients to cover up the rest of the meat
  5. cover the meat with 1 tablespoon of Adobo and 1 teaspoon on the skin
  6. use 1 packet of Sason seasoning on the meat and 1 packet on the skin
  7. cover pork in baking pan tightly in aluminum foil and place in refridgerator overnight
  8. heat over to 350 degrees while leaving roast pork outside of fridge for around 20 minutes
  9. leave the pork in oven covered for 1 hour per pound. For example, 8 lb. roast pork should stay covered in oven for 8 hours
  10. Remove pork from oven while leaving it at 350 degrees, remove aluminum foil cover on top of pork and put back in oven for 20 more minutes
