Roast Ham

Description

this is a roast ham I made previously for Chistmas for my relatives. I was able to find the recipe from this website ham

There are a couple of ingredient differences between what I used and what the website suggests. While I used orange juice, which is suggeted if preferred over pineapple juice, I used ground cinnamon instead of ground ginger and used honey mustard instead of dijon mustard.

Ingredients for Ham:

  • ham
  • 2 tablespoons honey mustard
  • 1/4 cup of orange juice

Ingredients for glaze:

  • 1/2 cup of orange juice
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons honey mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons of cornstarch

instructions

  1. preheat oven to 325 degrees
  2. combine honey mustard and orange juice and brush over ham
  3. place ham meat side down in baking pan and cover in aluminum foil
  4. cook for 15 minutes per pound
  5. while ham is cooking, mix glaze ingredients in pot and cook until it boils. remove from heat once finished
  6. When the ham is 15 minutes away from being finished, remove from oven and raise temperature to 425 degrees
  7. remove foil from top of ham, brush glaze all over ham, and place back in oven for remaining 15 minutes
